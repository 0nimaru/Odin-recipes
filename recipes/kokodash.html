Kokodash

Kokodahs is a traditional albanian dish for the poor yet very rich in flavor

  • bread
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Olive Oil
  1. Cut the bread in very small pieces
  2. Cut the cheese in small pieces
  3. Add them together in the plate
  4. Add the chopped tomatos
  5. Add the olive oil while stiring the bread cheese and tomatos
  6. Voila
